Roasted Groundnut

About This Recipe

Roasted Groundnut (peanuts) is a crunchy and flavorful snack commonly enjoyed in Ghana. It can be eaten alone or paired with roasted maize, bread, or used in various dishes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups raw groundnuts (peanuts)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1/4 cup water (if using salt)

Instructions

  1. Preheat a pan over medium heat or preheat an oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Rinse the raw groundnuts and drain well.
  3. Optional: Dissolve salt in water and mix with the groundnuts for a salted taste.
  4. Spread the groundnuts in a single layer on a baking tray or pan.
  5. Roast in the oven for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally, or stir-fry in a dry pan over medium heat for about 10–15 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Let cool before serving. Store in an airtight container.
