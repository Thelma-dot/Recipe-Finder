Cassava Chips

About This Recipe

Cassava Chips are a crunchy and savory snack made from thinly sliced cassava, deep-fried to a crispy perfection. These chips are a popular snack in many African countries and can be enjoyed on their own or with a dip.

Ingredients

  • 2 large cassava roots
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Peel the cassava roots and slice them thinly using a knife or mandoline slicer.
  2. In a bowl, mix the cassava slices with salt and optional paprika.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  4. Carefully add the cassava slices in batches and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove from oil and drain on a paper towel.
  6. Let them cool completely before serving or storing in an airtight container.
