Roasted Maize

About This Recipe

Roasted Maize is a popular Ghanaian street food, enjoyed as a simple and delicious snack. It is often paired with coconut or groundnuts (peanuts) for extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh maize (corn on the cob)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • Groundnuts (peanuts) or coconut (for serving, optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat a charcoal grill or stovetop grill pan over medium heat.
  2. Remove the husks from the maize and rinse with water.
  3. Place the maize directly on the grill.
  4. Turn the maize every 3–5 minutes to ensure even roasting.
  5. Roast for about 15–20 minutes until the kernels are golden brown and slightly charred.
  6. Sprinkle with a little salt if desired.
  7. Serve hot with groundnuts (peanuts) or fresh coconut slices.
