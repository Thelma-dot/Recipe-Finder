Plantain Chips

About This Recipe

Plantain Chips are a crispy and delicious Ghanaian snack made from unripe or slightly ripe plantains. They are often seasoned with salt and can be enjoyed on the go.

Ingredients

  • 2 large unripe or slightly ripe plantains
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional, for spice)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Instructions

  1. Peel the plantains and slice them thinly using a knife or a mandoline slicer.
  2. In a bowl, mix the plantain slices with salt and optional cayenne pepper.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  4. Carefully add the plantain slices in batches and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Remove from oil and drain on a paper towel.
  6. Let them cool completely before serving or storing in an airtight container.
