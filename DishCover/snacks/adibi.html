Adibi Agbeli

About This Recipe

Adibi Agbeli is a traditional Ewe dish made from fermented cassava dough. It has a smooth, slightly tangy taste and is commonly served with soups or light stews.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fermented cassava dough
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, mix the fermented cassava dough with water until smooth.
  2. Place the pan over medium heat and stir continuously.
  3. Cook for about 10-15 minutes, stirring constantly to prevent lumps.
  4. Once it thickens and becomes stretchy, remove from heat.
  5. Shape into small balls or serve as desired.
  6. Enjoy with soup, light soup, or palm nut soup.
