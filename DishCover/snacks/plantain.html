Roasted Plantain

About This Recipe

Roasted Plantain, locally known as "Kofi Brokeman" in Ghana, is a popular street food made by grilling ripe or semi-ripe plantains over an open flame. It is often enjoyed with roasted groundnuts (peanuts) for a simple yet delicious snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe or semi-ripe plantains
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground chili powder (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1/2 cup roasted groundnuts (peanuts), for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat a charcoal grill, gas grill, or oven to medium heat.
  2. Peel the plantains and lightly sprinkle with salt and chili powder if desired.
  3. Place the plantains on the grill and roast, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and slightly charred (about 10-15 minutes).
  4. Remove from the grill and allow them to cool slightly.
  5. Serve warm with roasted groundnuts.
  6. Enjoy as a delicious and nutritious street snack!
