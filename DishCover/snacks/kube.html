Kube Cake

About This Recipe

Kube Cake is a delicious Ghanaian dessert made with rice flour, sugar, and coconut. It's soft, moist, and sweet, making it a perfect treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water (or as needed)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rice flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and grated coconut.
  3. Add the coconut milk, vanilla extract, and water. Stir until the batter is smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. Allow the Kube Cake to cool before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
