Fried Yam

About This Recipe

Fried Yam is a delicious and crispy Ghanaian street food often served with pepper sauce (shito), fried fish, or grilled chicken. It is similar to French fries but made from yam.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized yam
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)
  • Water (for soaking)

Instructions

  1. Peel the yam and cut it into thick or thin slices, depending on preference.
  2. Rinse the yam slices in water and soak for about 5 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain and pat dry with a clean kitchen towel.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Sprinkle a little salt over the yam slices.
  6. Carefully add the yam slices to the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from oil and drain on a paper towel.
  8. Serve hot with pepper sauce (shito), fried fish, or grilled chicken.
