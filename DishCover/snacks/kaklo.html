Agbeli Kaklo

About This Recipe

Agbeli Kaklo is a crispy Ghanaian snack made from grated cassava. It is commonly enjoyed with coconut and is popular among the Ewe people of Ghana.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated cassava
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Vegetable oil (for deep frying)
  • Coconut chunks (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Peel and grate the cassava, then squeeze out excess water using a clean cloth.
  2. In a bowl, mix the grated cassava with salt and ginger.
  3. Shape the mixture into small balls or discs.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Fry the cassava balls until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels.
  7. Serve warm with fresh coconut slices.
