Abele

About This Recipe

Abele Walls is a popular Ghanaian frozen treat, often made from milk, sugar, and flavors like chocolate, vanilla, or fruit juice. It is typically poured into small plastic bags or molds and frozen until solid.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups evaporated milk
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder (for chocolate flavor, optional)
  • 1/2 cup fruit puree (optional for fruity flavors)
  • 1 cup water
  • Small plastic bags or ice molds

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the evaporated milk, condensed milk, sugar, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  2. If making chocolate Abele Walls, dissolve cocoa powder in warm water and mix it into the milk mixture.
  3. If making a fruit-flavored version, blend your chosen fruit into a puree and mix it in.
  4. Pour the mixture into small plastic bags or ice molds, leaving some space at the top.
  5. Seal the bags tightly or cover the molds and freeze for at least 4-6 hours, or until completely solid.
  6. Once frozen, remove from the freezer and enjoy your homemade Abele Walls!
