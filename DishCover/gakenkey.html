Ga Kenkey

Home All Recipes

About This Recipe

Ga Kenkey is a traditional fermented corn dough dish from the Greater Accra region of Ghana. It is often served with pepper sauce, fried fish, and shito.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups corn dough
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • Banana leaves or corn husks (for wrapping)

Instructions

  1. Mix the corn dough with salt and a little water to form a soft dough.
  2. Divide the dough into portions and wrap each portion in banana leaves or corn husks.
  3. Steam for about 1.5 to 2 hours until firm.
  4. Serve hot with pepper sauce, fried fish, or shito.
