Tiger Nut

About This Drink

Tiger Nut Drink, also known as Atadwe Milk, is a naturally sweet and creamy beverage made from tiger nuts. It is a popular, nutritious drink in Ghana, rich in fiber and essential nutrients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried tiger nuts (soaked overnight)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup dates (pitted, optional for sweetness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger (grated, optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • Honey or sugar (to taste, optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the soaked tiger nuts thoroughly.
  2. Add the tiger nuts, water, dates, and ginger to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Strain the mixture using a fine sieve or cheesecloth to extract the liquid.
  5. Add vanilla extract, cinnamon, and honey (if using) and stir well.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour before serving.
  7. Serve cold and enjoy!
