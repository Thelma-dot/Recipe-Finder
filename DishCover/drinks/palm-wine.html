Palm Wine

About This Drink

Palm Wine is a natural, mildly alcoholic drink tapped from palm trees. It is widely enjoyed in Ghana and across West Africa, often served fresh or fermented.

Ingredients

  • Freshly tapped palm sap (from oil palm or raffia palm trees)

Instructions

  1. Locate a mature oil palm or raffia palm tree.
  2. Make a small incision in the tree and insert a clean gourd or container to collect the sap.
  3. Allow the sap to drip into the container over several hours.
  4. For a fresh, mildly sweet taste, serve the palm wine immediately.
  5. For a stronger, fermented version, leave the palm wine at room temperature for 24–48 hours.
  6. Chill before serving for a refreshing drink.
