Orange Juice

About This Recipe

Fresh orange juice is a refreshing and nutritious drink made from freshly squeezed oranges. It is rich in vitamin C and perfect for hydration.

Ingredients

  • 4 large oranges
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water (optional, for dilution)
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Mint leaves (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash the oranges thoroughly and cut them in half.
  2. Use a citrus juicer or manually squeeze the juice into a bowl.
  3. Strain the juice to remove pulp and seeds (optional).
  4. Add honey if desired and stir well.
  5. For a chilled drink, add ice cubes or refrigerate before serving.
  6. Garnish with mint leaves and serve fresh.
