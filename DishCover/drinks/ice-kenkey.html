Ice Kenkey

About This Recipe

Ice Kenkey is a delicious Ghanaian chilled drink made from mashed kenkey, milk, sugar, and sometimes groundnuts. It is creamy, refreshing, and perfect for hot days.

Ingredients

  • 2 balls of kenkey (preferably Ga or Fante kenkey)
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence (optional)
  • 1/2 cup groundnuts (peanuts), crushed (for garnish)
  • Ice cubes (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Peel the kenkey and break it into smaller pieces in a large bowl.
  2. Add 1 cup of water and mash the kenkey using clean hands or a blender until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the remaining water while stirring to get a thick but smooth consistency.
  4. Stir in the evaporated milk, sugar, and vanilla essence (if using).
  5. Blend or whisk well to make it frothy.
  6. Refrigerate or serve immediately over ice cubes.
  7. Garnish with crushed groundnuts before serving.
