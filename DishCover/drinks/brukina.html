Brukina

About This Drink

Brukina, also known as "Deger," is a refreshing Ghanaian drink made from fermented millet and milk. It is a nutritious and filling beverage enjoyed chilled.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups millet
  • 4 cups water (for soaking and cooking)
  • 2 cups evaporated milk (or fresh milk)
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk (optional, for sweetness)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash the millet thoroughly and soak it in water for 4–6 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain the water and blend the millet into a smooth paste.
  3. Cook the blended millet over medium heat while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Once thickened, allow it to cool completely.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the evaporated milk, condensed milk (if using), sugar, salt, and vanilla extract.
  6. Add the cooled cooked millet into the milk mixture and stir well.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
  8. Serve chilled in a glass and enjoy!
