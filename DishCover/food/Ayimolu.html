Ayimolu

About This Recipe

Ayimolu is a nutritious Ghanaian dish made from black-eyed peas and corn. It is often served as a porridge and enjoyed as a hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried black-eyed peas
  • 2 cups dried corn (hominy corn or cracked corn)
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda (optional, to soften corn faster)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger (optional, for extra flavor)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar or honey (optional, for sweetness)

Instructions

  1. Soak the black-eyed peas and dried corn in water overnight.
  2. Rinse and drain the soaked beans and corn.
  3. In a large pot, add the corn, 3 cups of water, and baking soda (if using), and cook over medium heat for about 30 minutes.
  4. Add the black-eyed peas and 1 more cup of water, then continue cooking until both the peas and corn are soft.
  5. Stir occasionally, adding more water if needed to prevent burning.
  6. Once fully cooked, season with salt and ginger (if using).
  7. Serve warm, either plain or with sugar/honey for sweetness.
