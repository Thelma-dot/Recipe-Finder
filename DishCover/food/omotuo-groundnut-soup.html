Omotuo & Groundnut Soup

About This Recipe

Sobolo, also known as hibiscus tea, is a refreshing Ghanaian drink made from dried hibiscus petals. It has a tart, fruity flavor and is often infused with pineapple, ginger, and spices for extra taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried hibiscus petals (sobolo leaves)
  • 1 medium pineapple (peeled and blended)
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cloves (optional)
  • 1 cinnamon stick (optional)
  • 5 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the dried hibiscus petals to remove any dust or particles.
  2. In a large pot, bring 5 cups of water to a boil.
  3. Add the hibiscus petals, blended pineapple, grated ginger, cloves, and cinnamon stick.
  4. Let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes until the color is deep red and flavors are well infused.
  5. Remove from heat and let it cool completely.
  6. Strain the mixture using a fine sieve or cheesecloth to remove solids.
  7. Stir in sugar or honey to taste.
  8. Refrigerate until chilled or serve immediately over ice cubes.
