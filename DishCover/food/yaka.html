Yake Yake

About This Recipe

Yake Yake is a traditional Ghanaian dish made from steamed cassava granules. It is similar to couscous and is often enjoyed with stew, sauce, or grilled fish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cassava granules (gari)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil or butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, sprinkle water over the cassava granules while stirring to moisten them.
  2. Let it sit for about 5 minutes to absorb the moisture.
  3. Heat a steamer or line a pot with banana leaves or a clean cloth.
  4. Transfer the moistened cassava granules into the steamer and cover.
  5. Steam for about 15-20 minutes until soft and fluffy.
  6. Once cooked, fluff with a fork and mix in vegetable oil or butter for extra flavor.
  7. Serve warm with stew, grilled fish, or shito (black pepper sauce).
