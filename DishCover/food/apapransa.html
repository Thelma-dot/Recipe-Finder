Apapransa

About This Recipe

Apapransa is a traditional Ghanaian dish made from roasted cornmeal, palm nut soup, and protein-rich ingredients. It is a hearty and flavorful meal enjoyed in many homes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups roasted cornmeal
  • 2 cups palm nut soup
  • 1/2 pound meat (chicken, beef, or goat)
  • 1/2 cup smoked fish (flaked)
  • 1/4 cup ground crayfish or dried shrimp
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2 fresh tomatoes (blended)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger (grated)
  • 1 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons palm oil
  • 1 teaspoon shrimp seasoning (optional)
  • 2 boiled eggs (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat palm oil and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add the blended tomatoes and cook for about 5 minutes.
  3. Add meat, smoked fish, ground crayfish, and seasoning. Stir well.
  4. Pour in the palm nut soup and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Gradually add the roasted cornmeal while stirring continuously to prevent lumps.
  6. Cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens.
  7. Let it cook for another 10 minutes, ensuring a smooth consistency.
  8. Adjust salt and seasoning to taste.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with boiled eggs.
