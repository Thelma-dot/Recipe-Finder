Bambara Beans

About This Recipe

Bambara beans, also known as Aboboi, are a protein-rich legume commonly eaten in Ghana. They are often boiled until soft and served with fried plantains, gari, or palm oil.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bambara beans
  • Water (for soaking and boiling)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground pepper (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons palm oil (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the Bambara beans and soak them in water overnight.
  2. Drain the beans and place them in a pot with fresh water.
  3. Boil for about 2-3 hours until the beans become soft.
  4. Add salt, chopped onions, and ground pepper.
  5. Drizzle with palm oil for extra flavor, if desired.
  6. Serve hot with fried plantains, gari, or alone as a nutritious meal.
